How it works

Tackling Covid-19 with community, we match the needs of front-line practioners with community-sourced direct donations.

If you are a medical provider or practitioner, make a request for needed supplies here. Donors will ship requested equipment directly to you.

If you are able to donate medical supplies, first, let us know what you have available, next the system will match to providers and share detailed information about how and where to ship your donation.
